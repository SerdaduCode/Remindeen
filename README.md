# Remindeen
## Prayer reminders and daily inspiration while you browse
Remindeen is a browser extension specifically designed to be a
prayer reminder and source of inspiration for Muslims in carrying
out their daily activities.

### V.1.0.0
- Initial release with basic reminder functionalities and features a user-friendly interface.
### V.1.1.0
- Add some UI improvements and bug fixes.
### V.1.2.0
- Adjustment to the reminder scheduling algorithm for better performance and reliability.
### V.1.3.0
- Add alarm sound and snooze feature for reminders, allowing users to customize their reminder experience and improve usability.
### V.1.4.0
- Add support for multiple languages, making the extension more accessible to a wider audience and enhancing user experience for non-English speakers.
### V.1.5.0
- Add side panel with support section, an easy way to support the development of the extension.
### V.1.6.0
- Add support for dark mode, providing users with a more comfortable browsing experience in low-light environments and enhancing the overall aesthetics of the extension.
### V.1.7.0
- Time Tracker to record the time spent on each website, allowing users to monitor their browsing habits and make informed decisions about their online activities.
- TIme Tracker powered by AI to classify the websites into categories such as work, entertainment, social media, etc., providing users with insights into how they spend their time online and helping them manage their digital well-being more effectively.
### V.1.8.0
- Charts and statistics to visualize the time spent on different categories of websites, giving users a clear overview of their browsing patterns and helping them identify areas where they can improve their time management.
### V.1.9.0
- Add Avatar named "Hanif" animation to enhance the user experience and make the extension more engaging and visually appealing.
